General Duas
لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدُ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيْكَ لَبَّيْكَ
Labbaik Allahumma labbaik, labbaika la sharika laka labbaik, innal-hamda wan-ni'mata laka wal-mulk, la sharika lak
Here I am at Your service, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, there is no partner for You, here I am. Verily all praise is for You, and every bounty is from You, and all dominion is Yours, there is no partner for You.

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
La ilaha illallah wahdahu la sharika lah, lahul-mulk wa lahul-hamd wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir
There is no deity except Allah, alone, without any partner. To Him belongs the sovereignty and to Him belongs all praise, and He is over all things omnipotent

Subhanalladhi sakhkhara lana hadha wa ma kunna lahu muqrinin wa inna ila rabbina lamunqalibun
Glory be to Him Who has subjected this to us, and we ourselves were not capable of it. And to our Lord we shall surely return
